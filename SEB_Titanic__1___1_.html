DATA ANALYSIS ON TITANIC¶

Introduction¶

Titanic was a British passenger ship that sank oin the north Atlantic ocean on April 15th,1912. It collided with an iceberg on its way to NewYork city from Southampton. There were 2224 passengers and crew aboard the ship. Given is the data set including the details of 891 passengers which we are going to analyze.
